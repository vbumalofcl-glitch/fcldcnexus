Notes

Masterlist logic: reflects all company-owned items from the warehouse and project sites.

Warehouse to Site: deducts from main warehouse and adds to project site balance.

Direct Site Delivery: adds to project site balance and to company masterlist.

Client Supplied: tracked separately and not added into company-owned masterlist.

Site Alert %: triggers Low Stock Watch when a site balance falls at or below your chosen remaining percentage of total company stock.

Site Monitoring: records total delivered, remaining stock, total used, and last stock count per project until completion.

Item Code Rule: generated as Category initials + Item initial + 01-99 sequence, e.g. Hand Tools + Hammer 5lbs = HT-H-01.

Warranty & Minimum Stock: warranty applies only to equipment, electrical tools, and electronic tools. Minimum stock applies only to materials and is automatically enforced at 10% minimum.
